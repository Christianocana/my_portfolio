Guess Quest

Type: Group Project
Timeline: Oct-Dec 2024

Me and two other students created a game called Guess Quest as our custom project for one of my classes. In Guess Quest, players take turns to find items in nodes at certain locations. Players can find good items and bad items in each of the nodes, and players can gain points for finding the good items. Players can take turns to guess, move to different locations according to the map, use hints to identify what remains in the game, and to quit out of the game. The game and its attributes are created by a hosting client and acts as the host server for the game so that other clients can connect to it by using a three-way handshake, allowing the game to run on a TCP connection of the players, and lets players take action when it is their turn and prevents them from taking action when it is not their turn. This video demonstrates more of what to expect once the program starts running.
